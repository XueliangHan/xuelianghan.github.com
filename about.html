Answer to the question - "Why this site looks so simple, even ugly?"
As a information website, the whole site is an instance of pursuing Simplicity. One thing I found out is that low quality information on the Internet are way too much more than anywhere else, We all have limited time, so, I won't waste my words and your time. I'll keep everything Simple and Stupid, so you can find the information you want in a very short time, and then leave this site to do other important things. By the way, I don't think it's ugly, I'll say it's Minimalism.
