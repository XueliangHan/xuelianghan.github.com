About this website - Legacy and Sharing
  • Honestly, I build this website mostly for selfish reason, I need a way to keep track of my things - whatever I am thinking about or working on. I research things and document my research with a post, which I can easily find and refer to later. Hopefully, other people will find these posts helpful, relevant, or interesting.
  • For the strong desire of Sharing, I built 2 personal site before, this site is my third generation personal publishing platform (Actually 3.5th generation), also to be an example of "The Hacker's Way" to publishing.
    • Answer to the question - "Why this site looks so ugly?"
    As a information website, the whole site is an instance of pursuing Simplicity. One thing I found out is that low quality information on the Internet are way too much more than anywhere else, We all have limited time, so, I won't waste my words and your time. I'll keep everything Simple and Stupid, so you can find the information you want in a very short time, and then leave this site to do other important things. By the way, I don't think it's ugly, I'll say it's Minimalism.
